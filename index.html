About Me

Hi, I'm Rachel. I studied AI and cognitive neuroscience in Spain and the US before joining Prof. Dr. Jakob Macke's lab "Machine Learning in Science" as a PhD student in September 2021. I am working on the DeepHumanVision project, using deep neural networks to study how populations of neurons encode visual information in the human brain. I am co-advised by Prof. Dr. Dr. Florian Mormann.
